MCC's comprehensive publishing services cover editing, design, and printing, ensuring a polished final product.

During my part-time period at Modern Creative Centre, I skillfully took on various roles. As a graphics designer, I brought a touch of visual excellence to projects. I also managed data entry tasks with great attention to detail. Creating captivating book covers and impactful logos brought an artistic flair to my tasks. This diverse experience not only improved my skills but also gave me a deeper understanding of different creative aspects. My time at Modern Creative Centre was a rewarding journey that enriched my versatility and skills.
